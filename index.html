Zanzibar Paradise Island !

Just off the mainland of Tanzania, on the east coast of Africa, is an Indian Ocean nirvana called Zanzibar. The Zanzibar archipelago is also known as the 'Spice Islands' because of its abundance of produce, spice, and clove plantations.

Zanzibar is blessed with beautiful islands, alluring seas, colorful corals, and exotic sea creatures, making it the perfect holiday in the sun.
